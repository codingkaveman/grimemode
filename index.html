Grime Mode

Born in Detroit, the birthplace of Techno, Nicholas Coombs - better known as Grime Mode - began playing music in 1995, when he received his first acoustic guitar for Christmas. From that moment on, life was about the music. Fast forward to 2006, Grime Mode attended Berklee College of Music for Guitar Performance. After touring with various metal bands, Grime Mode began producing and playing his own music in 2013.

In 2008, Grime Mode first discovered dubstep and immediately fell in love with the sounds of Skream, Benga, Hatcha, Youngsta, and many other pioneers of the genre. Coming from a Hardcore, Deathcore, and D-Jent Metal background, Grime Mode has always pushed the heavier sounds of music into his original music, remixes, and live DJ sets. Grime Mode is known for his brutal Live Edits you won't hear anywhere else!

Grime Mode is currently working on recording live guitars, basses, and in the future, you can expect to hear tons of live instrument recordings in his original music and remixes. With his first love and first instrument being guitars, Grime Mode is looking to incorporate a Live 7 String Guitar into live performances soon!
